Understanding Fibonacci Retracement

Fibonacci Retracement is a powerful technical analysis tool used to identify potential support and resistance levels based on the Fibonacci sequence.

Key Fibonacci Levels:

  • 23.6% - Shallow retracement
  • 38.2% - Moderate retracement
  • 50.0% - Psychological midpoint
  • 61.8% - Golden ratio (most important)
  • 78.6% - Deep retracement
Fibonacci Retracement Support Levels Resistance Levels Price Action

Choosing a Regulated Broker

⚠️

Important: Always verify regulatory status before opening an account!

What to Look For:

  • ✓ Proper regulatory license (SEC, FCA, ASIC, CySEC, etc.)
  • ✓ Segregated client funds
  • ✓ Negative balance protection
  • ✓ Transparent fee structure
  • ✓ Strong customer support
  • ✓ User-friendly trading platform

Regulatory Bodies to Check:

🇺🇸 United States: SEC, CFTC, NFA
🇬🇧 United Kingdom: FCA
🇦🇺 Australia: ASIC
🇪🇺 European Union: CySEC, BaFin, CONSOB

Never trade with unregulated brokers. Always verify the broker's license number on the regulator's official website. If something seems too good to be true, it probably is.
